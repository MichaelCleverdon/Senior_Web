How I installed Mongo and use it in NodeJS

These are my notes from Mongo.

    #### Delete Many ####

db.customers.deleteMany({first_name: "Brielle"});

### Update ###

db.customers.update({first_name: "John"}, {first_name: "John", last_name: "Houston", gender:"Male"});

#Works for many Johns, not just the first one
db.customers.updateMany({first_name: "John"}, {first_name: "John", last_name: "Houston", gender:"Male"});

                       $set works to keep everything the same except what is in the second curleys

db.customers.update({first_name: "Brielle"}, {$set:{gender:"Female"}});




################## Increment ##################

Effectively the + version for ints in mongo

$inc:{[field]:howMuchToIncrement}
ex:
db.customers.update({first_name: "John"}, {$inc: {age:5}});
adds 5 to John's age

########## Removing columns ##########

$unset:{[column]:1}

ex:
db.customers.update({first_name: "John"}, {$unset:{age:1}};
Removes age field for first 'John';


######### Rename #########

$rename:{"[initial_name]":"[renamed_name]"}});

ex:
db.customers.update({first_name: "John"}, {$rename:{"gender":"sex"}});



##### Remove record ######

db.customers.remove({first_name:"John", last_name:"Smith"});

Shouldn't use name, because names can be duplicated
